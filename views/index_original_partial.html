Why use BookStack?

Someone on campus is looking for your book

Every quarter begins with the same process, hunting down a textbook your class requires in the bookstore and everywhere online. However, there is someone who just finished that same class and no longer needs the textbook. That's why we created BookStack: to help UCLA students connect with one another and negotiate a beneficial price for both sides.
